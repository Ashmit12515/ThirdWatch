Northstar Health Cloud
SOC 2 Type II Examination Report Summary
Reporting Period: January 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025

Security Controls

Northstar Health Cloud maintains a current SOC 2 Type II report.

Multi-factor authentication is required for all privileged accounts,
administrative users, production access, and remote access.

All customer data and backups are protected using AES-256 encryption at rest.

An incident response plan is documented, tested annually, and reviewed
following material security events.

The organization maintains ISO 27001 certification for its information
security management system.

Subprocessors are reviewed annually and are subject to contractual
security and privacy requirements.
